Instructions

Welcome to the Real-time Motion Monitoring system. This tool helps you monitor motion in a specified area through your camera feed. Follow the instructions below to get started:

1. Ensure your camera is connected and properly configured.

2. Enter your email address in the input field provided and click on "Activate Motion Detector" to start receiving motion alerts.

3. Once activated, the system will monitor for any movement and send you email notifications with snapshots of detected motion.

4. To switch between dark mode and light mode, click on the sun/moon icon in the navigation bar.

For any issues or further assistance, please refer to the help documentation or contact support.

Thank you for using our Real-time Motion Monitoring system!
